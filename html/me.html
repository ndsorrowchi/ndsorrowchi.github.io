Academics

I like to study information science and technology because I think that's not only part of future, but also our thoughts about ourselves. We are not only learning how to code, how to build websites, how to write servers. More beyond that, we learn how people process Information. We learn why things are disigned like that, why people understand how to use a tool even they have not used it before. And, inforamtion technologies will sure continue to change people's life in the future.

Also, I like GIS because we all need to process some kind of spatial information today. We ask want to know where things are, where we're going. We need to know what a place will be like after several years. These are all involved in GIS. GIS is applied in everywhere in everyday life.
